About

Raisa Ihnat is a Ukrainian-American interdisciplinary musician and multimedia artist based in New York City and central New Jersey. Her work is grounded in inspiration from diverse sources — nature, color, synthesis, and the quiet playfulness of everyday life. She is a recent graduate of Trinity College Dublin (M.Phil) and Skidmore College (B.A) where she primarily studied music, continuing her advanced studies in classical piano as well as expanding her creative practice into multimedia technologies and audio engineering. Aside from the arts, you'll find her foraging, hiking, cooking, and playing with her cat Saja.
